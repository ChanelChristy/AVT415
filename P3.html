User Scenario Mapping

Scenario

Blair is a college student who majors in Fashion at the Fashion Institue of Technology in New York. She had to put together a fashion show for a final project showcasing rare and vintage clothing items, but she put it off until last minute and didn't have time to go to countless stores and look for these items. She looked online and found "Timeless." An online vintage clothing store that had the option to buy or rent clothes, alonng with a delivery or pick-up in person option. She browses the online store and finds exactly what she needs for her final project. By doing so she saved a ton of time, was relieved to find one place that had everything she needed, and was able to present her final project in time.

Actor

Blair

Motivator

Needing to complete her final project on time.

Intention

Blair needs to find a way to save travel time of going to multiple stores to find things and save time looking through countless items in stores.

Action

Blair starts looking online for stores near her that sell vintage clothing items. She searches for stores that have a variety of brands. Blair comes across Timeless, an online vintage clothing store.

Resolution

Blair browses the store for the items she needs and places a delivery order for them, saving herself a lot of time and having her final project ready and on the way.
